About REZCA

Small businesses deserve agency-grade marketing.

REZCA exists because too many small businesses are stuck with an outdated website, marketing that doesn't talk to each other, and no real system behind any of it. We're here to fix that.

Why we exist

Most agencies sell you a piece. We build the whole system.

A designer builds you a site. A separate freelancer runs your ads. Nobody touches your backend. Meanwhile, you're the one stuck stitching it all together: replying to reviews, drafting emails, chasing leads in a spreadsheet.

REZCA was built to close that gap: one team that builds your website, integrates the AI tools that save you hours every week, and connects the backend so everything actually works together.

How we operate

The approach behind every build.

01

Listen first

Every engagement starts with your story: your brand voice, your "why," and how your business actually runs day to day. We don't template that away.

02

Diagnose honestly

We tell you what's actually broken (a slow site, weak SEO, a Google profile nobody's managing) instead of just pitching services.

03

Build it connected

Website, AI, and backend are treated as one system from day one, not three separate projects that never talk to each other.

04

Stay accountable

We're a small, hands-on team, and every client gets direct access to the people actually doing the work.
